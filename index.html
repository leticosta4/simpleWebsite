The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo

Taylor Jenkings Reid

'The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo' follows aging Hollywood icon Evelyn Hugo as she reveals her scandalous life story to unknown magazine reporter Monique Grant. Monique, struggling in her personal and professional life, is surprised by Evelyn's choice and is determined to use this opportunity to kickstart her career. As Evelyn recounts her journey from the 1950s to the 1980s, including seven marriages and unexpected friendships, Monique becomes deeply connected to the star. However, as the story unfolds, it becomes evident that Evelyn's life is intertwined with Monique's in tragic and irreversible ways. Despite what the title may suggest, this novel doesn't really focus on the husbands, but rather Evelyn herself, where we quickly learn that there is much more to her than meets the eye.
